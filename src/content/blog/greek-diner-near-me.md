When searching for a Greek diner near me, you likely want something fresh, filling, and convenient for any time of day. At Kafenio Avondale, the focus is straightforward: quality food featuring bold Mediterranean flavors served in a welcoming atmosphere where you can genuinely enjoy your meal.

The restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner throughout the day, with all menu items emphasizing well-balanced dishes designed to satisfy without being overly heavy.

## A Greek Diner That Fits Your Day

Located in Avondale, the space is bright, clean, and welcoming. The restaurant opened in 2018 with the mission of serving quality food at reasonable prices in a comfortable setting — a philosophy that continues to guide everything on the menu.

All-day breakfast service allows customers to order their favorites whenever desired, not limited to morning hours.

## Fresh Greek Diner Favorites

- **Greek Salad** — romaine, feta, tomato, cucumber, red onion, kalamata olives, and pepperoncini
- **Horiatiki** — traditional village salad with vegetables, feta, olives, and lemon dressing (no lettuce)
- **Classic Gyro** — sliced gyro meat with tzatziki and feta spread, as a wrap or platter with sides
- **Chicken Souvlaki** — a lighter alternative with bold Mediterranean flavor
- **Falafel** — crispy, fresh, and satisfying
- **Spanakopita** — flaky phyllo with spinach-feta filling
- **Dolmades** — seasoned rice wrapped in grape leaves
- **Greek Sampler Platter** — a mix of starters combining flaky, tangy, creamy, and fresh elements
