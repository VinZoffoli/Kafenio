Looking for a Greek coffee shop in Decatur? Kafenio Avondale brings together strong coffee, fresh breakfast, and a laid-back space that fits naturally into your day. Whether stopping in for a quick espresso or sitting down for a full meal, everything centers on simple ingredients and bold Mediterranean flavor.

## Coffee, Espresso, and Greek Tradition

Coffee is central to the experience at Kafenio. Greek coffee is rich, strong, and meant to be enjoyed slowly. The full espresso menu includes espresso, cappuccino, cortadito, and lattes — with iced versions available. Drinks can be customized with vanilla, caramel, mocha, or honey syrup, and oat milk is always an option.

## Breakfast Sandwiches That Actually Fill You Up

All sandwiches are served on pita, bagel, toast, or gluten-free pita:

- **Ares** — fried egg, bacon, cheddar
- **Athena** — scrambled egg, feta, arugula, bell pepper, onion, tomato
- **Hera** — fried egg, sausage, feta spread
- **Artemis** — fried egg, souvlaki chicken, kasseri, harissa

Vegan sausage and tofu scramble substitutions are available.

## Breakfast Plates, Omelettes, and Sweet Options

Breakfast plates include eggs, choice of meat, Greek potatoes, and bread. Omelettes feature Mediterranean flavors — try the **Demeter** with chicken sausage, arugula, and feta, served with Greek potatoes and bread.

Sweet options include **Zeus French Toast** with Nutella, banana, pecans, and honey syrup; the **Aphrodite** with cherry compote and fresh blueberries; and Greek yogurt with house-made granola, banana, and fruit compote.

## Quick Bites and Sides

- Everything bagels with cream cheese, feta spread, or hummus
- Cinnamon rolls
- Greek yogurt with honey
- Greek potatoes or eggs

## Why This Greek Coffee Shop Works

Strong coffee with a full espresso menu, breakfast sandwiches built with fresh ingredients, dietary options including vegan substitutes, and a relaxed, welcoming space that fits any schedule.
