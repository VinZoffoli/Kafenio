A nutritious morning meal establishes momentum for the day ahead. At Kafenio Avondale, Mediterranean-inspired dishes, energizing coffee, and comforting classics come together in a space designed for mornings worth savoring.

The breakfast offering emphasizes flavor, balance, and connection — reflecting the Mediterranean way of starting the day with purpose and intention.

## Where Mediterranean Flavor Meets Morning Comfort

Kafenio reimagines the Greek diner concept for contemporary living. The approach to morning dining reflects Mediterranean eating customs: quality components, well-balanced tastes, and meals that nourish both physically and emotionally.

The modern diner atmosphere blends clean design with rustic touches, creating a relaxed setting to enjoy breakfast at your own pace. Since 2018, the restaurant has welcomed guests from Avondale, Decatur, and surrounding neighborhoods with the same warmth and hospitality.

## Fresh and Wholesome Breakfast Favorites

### Greek Omelette
A signature Mediterranean preparation with bold flavors, served with hashbrowns or a cucumber-tomato mixture and your choice of biscuit or pita bread.

### Breakfast Plate
Choose your protein — bacon, sausage, or chicken sausage — paired with eggs, hashbrowns or a vegetable accompaniment, and bread.

### French Toast
Thick-sliced bread soaked in a cinnamon vanilla mixture, garnished with powdered sugar and seasonal fruit. Served warm.

### Breakfast Grain Bowl
A nutrient-dense option combining sautéed grains with your protein choice, egg, vegetable components, and fresh tomato.

### Breakfast Sandwiches
Quick, nourishing options on your choice of pita, bagel, toast, or gluten-free pita. Built with fresh, bold Mediterranean ingredients.

## All-Day Breakfast, Your Way

Kafenio offers all-day breakfast service — enjoy your favorites at any hour, not just in the morning. Dine in, pick up, or order delivery. Whatever fits your day.
