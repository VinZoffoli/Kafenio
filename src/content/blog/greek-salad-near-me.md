If you're searching for a greek salad near me in Decatur, Kafenio Avondale offers crisp vegetables, bold Mediterranean flavor, and perfectly balanced portions — whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch or sitting down for a full meal.

Located at 2700 E College Ave #3000, the restaurant serves the local community with reliable, fresh food and an authentic Mediterranean menu.

## Fresh Greek Salads, Done Right

The **Greek/American Salad** features romaine lettuce, feta, tomato wedges, cucumber, onion, kalamata olives, and pepperoncini. The flavors are balanced and straightforward, combining the saltiness of feta and olives with crisp, fresh vegetables.

The **Greek Caesar Salad** offers romaine, pita croutons, feta, and Caesar dressing — savory and creamy while maintaining Greek character through the feta and fresh greens.

## Pair the Salads With Something More

- **Greek Potatoes** — served warm and seasoned, topped with feta and tzatziki
- **Dip Duo** — choose two spreads (tzatziki, hummus, feta spread, or melitzanosalata) served with pita and fresh cucumber
- **Spanakopita** — flaky phyllo with spinach-feta filling
- **Dolmades** — seasoned rice wrapped in grape leaves

## More Greek Flavors on the Menu

The **Gyro Platter** features shaved meat, pilaf, roasted Greek vegetables, pita, and tzatziki. The **Souvlaki Chicken Skewer Platter** includes grilled chicken, pilaf, roasted vegetables, and your choice of tzatziki or mustard sauce. For vegetarians, the **Papoutsakia** offers stuffed eggplant with tomato sauce, cheese, and béchamel.
